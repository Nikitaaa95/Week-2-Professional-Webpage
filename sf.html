San Francisco

Home base. The city that spoiled me for every other food scene.

📚 Do not skip this

City Lights Bookstore

I love this place. Genuinely one of my favorite spots in the city — three floors of independent bookstore energy, a legendary poetry room upstairs, and zero pressure to buy anything. The kind of place you wander into for 20 minutes and walk out two hours later.

Open until 10pm · Every single day

🌉 Eat & Drink

Zante's Pizza curry pizza

Yes, curry pizza. It sounds like it shouldn't work and it absolutely does. An SF original — don't leave without trying it.

El Farolito burrito, obviously

A classic. You're in California — you better be getting a good burrito. This is the one.

Any bakery get the dutch crunch

Everyone talks about sourdough (and yes, get the sourdough), but dutch crunch is the real local secret. That crunchy, crackly roll is an SF thing and you will not find it anywhere else.

Local Edition the Eva Gardner

Gorgeous newspaper-themed bar in the basement of an old Hearst building. Order the Eva Gardner. You're welcome.
