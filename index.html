About Me

I'm an MBA candidate at UC Berkeley Haas (Class of 2027) with 8 years of experience leading technology integrations, workflow automation, and cross-functional platform projects in financial services. My career has spanned JP Morgan and First Republic Securities, where I held progressively senior operations roles — most recently as Senior Director.

I'm passionate about the intersection of business operations and technology.
